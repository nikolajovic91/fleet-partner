Additional Services

Damage Control

Our company fully takes on the entire process of vehicle damage control: establishing contact with insurance companies; contracting vehicle repairs with service workshops; returning the vehicle to the client. During the repair period we ensure the mobility of the client or vehicle user with the replacement vehicle service.

VIP Services

To our VIP clients we offer tailor made solutions. Door to door service which includes picking up and returning the vehicle for the purpose of vehicle maintenance. We are also able to provide all other VIP services on request by prior arrangement.

Other Services

In addition to the above mentioned services, we are able to offer all clients a variety of additional services:

Equipping vehicles with satellite GPS devices,

Vehicle branding,

Additional vehicle upgrades.
