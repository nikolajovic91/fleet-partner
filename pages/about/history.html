History

Fleet Partner was founded in 2014. as a company that will provide fleet management full service solution.

We planned to provide services in all fazes of vehicle life cycle. From vehicle purchasing and maintenance to remarketing solutions.

In the first year, our focus was on remarketing. In accordance with our vision to become one of the best companies in this field we are striving to improve the quality of our services. That’s why we became a part of Element-Arval Global Alliance as 47th member in the world.

For us, it’s not enough to be the solution, we have to be the best solution!
