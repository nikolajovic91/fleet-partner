About the alliance

Element Arval Global Alliance drives forward businesses of companies all around the globe with the powerful portfolio of fleet services from Element Fleet Management. Always tuned in to their partners' emerging needs, members of the Alliance develop new products and customized solutions to help reduce costs and improve productivity.

Present in North America, Asia-Pacific and Africa, the Element Arval Global Alliance gathers the world’s best fleet management organizations and provides clients with the same expert advice and service quality no matter what country the client is located in.

Element Arval Global Alliance operates more than 3.000.0000 vehicles globally.
